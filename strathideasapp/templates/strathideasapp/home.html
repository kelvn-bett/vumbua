ABOUT VUMBUA

Welcome to Vumbua, an Innovation Hub that offers a number of incubation programmes in the Bioeconomy (agro processing and pharmaceutical), Smart Industries (ICT and advanced manufacturing) and Green Economy (Water purification, waste management and renewable energy).

In addition, The Innovation Hub operates a range of enterprise development, skills development and innovation enabling programmes both in the science park and throughout Kenya

HOW VUMBUA OPERATES...

Strathmore university is an institution that is composed of mainly the students and the staff members. These two entities usually have a number of good ideas which are frequently thrown under the bus. This is mainly due to lack of resources and a platform to share their ideas and improve them given a certain way of scrutinizing them and in the end the final product is polished perfectly. Vumbua is a platform for sharing the ideas as the incubatees are mentored.
